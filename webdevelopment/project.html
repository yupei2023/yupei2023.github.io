Project VirtualGeo

Topic: Blending Mixed Reality and Generative AI to Teach Geography: An MR+GenAI Learning Environment

This study examines “VirtualGeo”, a Mixed Reality and Generative AI platform designed to enhance U.S. geography knowledge among international students. By integrating immersive technologies, VirtualGeo allows students to engage with spatial content within an interactive digital landscape. Findings show significant gains in international students’ geographic understanding and underscore VirtualGeo's potential to support adaptive, student-centered learning. Implications suggest VirtualGeo as a scalable tool for online training, promoting broader access to geography and other spatial disciplines, and highlighting new possibilities for research, policy, and practice in digital education.

Limitations and Future Directions

  • Small sample size limits generalizability.
  • Self-reported data may introduce bias.
  • Comfort and eye-strain issues affect usability.
  • Long-term retention not assessed.
  • Future work: larger diverse samples, longitudinal studies, mobile AR support.
